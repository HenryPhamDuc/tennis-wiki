# Lu Response Drill

Lu Response Drill là drill defend và redirect dưới heavy feed, dựa trên **Lu (Rollback)** principle từ [Tai Chi 8 Gates in Tennis](../ky-thuat/tai-chi-8-gates-in-tennis.md) — "yield vào incoming force, swallow nó vào core, redirect từ rooted base" — thay vì "fight back" bằng arm power.

> "**The 'Heavy Feed':** Have a partner or ball machine fire high-velocity balls deep into your corners. **The 'Lu' Response:** Your only goal is to return the ball high and deep (above the net strap) while maintaining a 2/10 grip pressure. If you tighten your grip, you lose the drill. **The 'Kinetic Freeze':** After the defensive hit, you must freeze your posture for 2 seconds. Are you balanced? Is your 'back leg' still rooted?"

---

## Cấu Trúc Ba Pha

### Phase 1 — Heavy Feed
Partner hoặc ball machine fires **high-velocity balls deep into corners** — không phải comfortable feed. Pressure là design element, không phải accident. Drill chỉ works với actual heavy balls vì:
- Light feed không trigger protective arm overtension.
- Không có heavy feed → không có real Lu practice — chỉ có choreography.

### Phase 2 — Lu Response (2/10 Grip Requirement)
**Mục tiêu duy nhất**: Return ball **high và deep (above net strap)** với grip pressure 2/10.

**Why high and deep**:
- High above net = defensive margin — không try to attack.
- Deep = neutralize opponent's next attack opportunity.
- "High and deep" là Lu's redirection output — không phải winner, không phải pace, mà là strategic reset.

**2/10 grip rule**: Nếu tighten grip → **lose the drill**.
- Tightening grip = arm starts fighting → Lu abandoned → Arming begins.
- 2/10 enforcement buộc player yield thay vì resist.

### Phase 3 — Kinetic Freeze (2 giây hold)
Sau hit, freeze posture hoàn toàn trong 2 giây. Check:
- "Are you balanced?"
- "Is your 'back leg' still rooted?"

**Lý do freeze**: Tư thế sau contact là evidence của root quality trong contact. Nếu player mất balance sau hit → root đã bị compromised trong contact — bóng trở lại tốt chỉ là may mắn, không phải skill.

---

## Correction Feedback

> "**If the ball flies short**, you are 'leaking' energy through a collapsed wrist. **If the ball flies long**, you are 'pushing' with the shoulder rather than 'yielding' with the core."

| Outcome | Diagnosis | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Ball too short** | Wrist collapsed → energy leaked before contact | Maintain 2/10 grip through contact point |
| **Ball too long** | Shoulder pushing → not yielding → linear force | Increase Lu yield — "swallow" more before redirect |
| **Ball high and deep** ✅ | Lu response correct — core redirecting | Maintain this |
| **Lost balance in freeze** | Root compromised during contact | Sink weight more before contact |

---

## "Lu" vs "Hitting Back"

| Response | Action | Kết quả |
|---|---|---|
| **Hitting back** | Arm fights incoming velocity | Co-contraction; timing disrupted; bóng short hoặc error |
| **Lu Response** | Yield, absorb into core, redirect | Minimal effort; bóng high and deep; rooted position maintained |

**"Swallowing" ball's energy**: Khi Lu is correct, player không phải generate lực bằng muscle — họ **redirect opponent's own pace** ngược lại. High-velocity incoming ball → more pace available để redirect → less muscular effort required.
