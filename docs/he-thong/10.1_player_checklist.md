# ✅ Final Checklist for the Player

## Pre-Match Checklist

- [ ] **Spine vertical during slide?** (DSI Check ≥ 0.85)
- [ ] **"Sink" during split-step?** (GSM Check, hạ 10% chiều cao)
- [ ] **Wrist locked at impact?** ($K_{wrist} \geq 0.85$)
- [ ] **Hitting into high-probability zone?** ($P_{error} < 40\%$)
- [ ] **HRV in Green zone?** (≥ baseline)
- [ ] **Fascia warmed up?** (Dynamic stretching done)

## During-Match Checklist

### Every Point

- [ ] Apply Neuro-Reset 16s?
- [ ] Grip pressure 3-4/10 (loose)?
- [ ] Eyes on ball until impact + 0.3s?

### After Every 3 Points

- [ ] HR > 180bpm? → Reset
- [ ] $\sigma_{sync}$ feeling shaky? → Slow down 1 point

## Per-Stroke Checklist

### Forehand

- [ ] Hip leads shoulder? (X-Factor)
- [ ] Wrist neutral (không drop)?
- [ ] Butt cap to ball?
- [ ] Weight transfer forward (chân sau kéo lê)?

### Backhand

- [ ] "See the watch"? (palm down)
- [ ] Coil trước khi bóng nảy?
- [ ] Thrust through ball?

### Serve

- [ ] GRF 2.3-2.5×BW ở Trophy Pose?
- [ ] Knee 100-120°?
- [ ] Shoulder < Hip at contact (X-Factor)
- [ ] Contact at highest point?

### Return

- [ ] Split-step đúng lúc?
- [ ] Backswing ≤ 20cm?
- [ ] $K_{wrist} \geq 0.95$?
- [ ] $\Delta t \leq 0.15s$?

### Volley

- [ ] V-step (45°)?
- [ ] Vợt KHÔNG ra sau vai?
- [ ] $K_{wrist} \geq 0.95$?
- [ ] Center dồn về phía trước?

## Post-Match Checklist

- [ ] HRV logged?
- [ ] Video uploaded cho AI analysis?
- [ ] Unforced errors categorized?
- [ ] Tomorrow's training plan (Xanh/Vàng/Đỏ)?
