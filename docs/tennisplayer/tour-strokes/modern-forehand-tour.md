# The Modern Tour Forehand

ATP forehands feature extreme grips (semi-western to western), heavy topspin (3,000+ rpm), and open stances. The kinetic chain generates 80+ mph racquet head speed. The windshield wiper finish and pat-the-dog low point are signature characteristics. Modern tour forehands are primarily open-stance, with closed stance used selectively.
