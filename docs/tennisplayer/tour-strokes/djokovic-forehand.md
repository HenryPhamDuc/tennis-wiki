# Djokovic's Defensive Forehand

Djokovic's forehand is a study in defensive excellence: extreme flexibility, incredible reach, and consistency under pressure. His stroke uses a semi-western grip, full kinetic chain, and remarkable balance. He generates heavy topspin from defensive positions, turning defense into offense through court positioning and counter-attack patterns.
