# The Drop Shot

The drop shot is a soft shot that just clears the net and bounces twice in the service box. Effective drop shots require disguise, touch, and the right tactical situation. Best used: when the opponent is deep behind the baseline, when the player has established a strong baseline game, and as a surprise rather than a pattern.
