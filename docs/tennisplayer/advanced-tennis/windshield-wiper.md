# The Windshield Wiper Forehand

The windshield wiper describes the modern forehand follow-through where the racquet brushes up the back of the ball and finishes across the body, like a windshield wiper. The motion is the natural result of proper sequencing and relaxed forearm pronation, not a forced shape. It generates heavy topspin and clean contact.
