# Pat the Dog — Forehand Slot Position

The 'pat the dog' position is the low point in the modern forehand's swing path, where the racquet head drops below the ball with the strings facing down. This position is gravity-assisted, not muscle-forced. From this low position, the racquet accelerates upward and forward, brushing up the back of the ball for topspin.
