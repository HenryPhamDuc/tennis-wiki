# Anticipation and Reading the Opponent

Anticipation is reading the opponent's body language, racquet preparation, and contact to predict the shot before it's hit. Elite players read the opponent's shoulders, hips, and racquet face angle to know where the ball is going. Practice: watch the opponent's body, not the ball.
