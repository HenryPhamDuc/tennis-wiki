# The Approach Shot

The approach shot sets up the net rush. It should be deep, well-placed, and have enough pace or spin to force a weak reply. Common approach patterns: deep crosscourt slice, deep body shot, or short angle. The shot quality is less important than the result: a reply you can volley.
