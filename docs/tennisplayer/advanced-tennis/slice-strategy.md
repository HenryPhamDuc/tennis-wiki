# The Slice in Modern Tennis

The slice (underspin) is a versatile shot that keeps the ball low, neutralizes pace, and disrupts rhythm. Used on the backhand as a defensive reset, the slice can also be a setup shot. On the forehand, the slice is less common but effective as a change of pace.
