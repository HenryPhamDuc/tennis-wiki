# The Crossover Step

The crossover step is an explosive lateral movement where the outside foot crosses in front of the inside foot. It allows the player to cover ground quickly to reach wide balls, far more efficient than shuffling. The hip rotation initiates the move, and the plant leg absorbs the deceleration.
