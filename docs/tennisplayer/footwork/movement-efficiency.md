# Movement Efficiency in Tennis

Movement efficiency means reaching the ball with minimum energy expenditure and maximum balance. Elite players use economy of motion: purposeful first step, efficient mid-court patterns, and quick recovery. Wasted movement (over-running, lazy recovery) costs energy and creates time pressure on every shot.
