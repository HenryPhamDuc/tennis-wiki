# The Shuffle Step

The shuffle step is a lateral movement pattern useful for short distances and maintaining balance. Unlike the crossover, shuffling keeps the feet parallel and is more efficient for small adjustments. The split-step leads into either a shuffle or a crossover depending on the ball's distance.
