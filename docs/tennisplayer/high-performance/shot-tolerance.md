# Shot Tolerance in Elite Tennis

Shot tolerance is the ability to handle extreme pace and spin without breaking down. Elite players develop this through progressive exposure to high-velocity balls, both in practice (with ball machines, hitting partners) and competition. Shot tolerance is built gradually, not overnight, and requires consistent technical foundation.
