# Performing Under High Pressure

High performance under pressure is the result of preparation, not talent. Players who have done the work in practice perform under pressure; players who haven't, don't. Building pressure performance: simulate pressure in practice, develop consistent routines, embrace pressure as part of the game, and trust the trained stroke.
