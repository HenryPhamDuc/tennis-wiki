# Athletic Development for Tennis

Tennis-specific athletic development combines strength, power, speed, agility, and flexibility. Key areas: rotational power (medicine ball throws), leg power (jumps, squats), agility (cone drills, ladder), and flexibility (dynamic stretching, yoga). Athletic development reduces injury risk and improves on-court performance.
