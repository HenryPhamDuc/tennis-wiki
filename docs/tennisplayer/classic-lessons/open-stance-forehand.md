# Open Stance Forehand

The open stance forehand allows full hip and shoulder rotation, generating power through separation and pelvic rotation rather than linear weight transfer. Modern baseline play relies on the open stance to handle high-bouncing balls and to recover quickly to the center. The outside leg is the key to loading and exploding into the shot.
