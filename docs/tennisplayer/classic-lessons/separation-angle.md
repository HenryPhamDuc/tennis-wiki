# Hip-Shoulder Separation (X-Factor)

The angle between the hips and shoulders during preparation creates elastic energy in the core. When the hips begin to open while the shoulders remain turned, the trunk stretches like a coiled spring. This stored elastic energy releases explosively at contact, adding power without muscular effort.
