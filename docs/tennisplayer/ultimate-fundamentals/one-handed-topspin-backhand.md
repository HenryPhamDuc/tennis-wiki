# The One-Handed Topspin Backhand

A high-risk, high-reward stroke requiring exceptional technique, timing, and strength. The one-handed topspin backhand opens up the court to attacking down-the-line shots and angles, but demands precise footwork and pre-loading the kinetic chain. Modern usage is rare at elite level, but the stroke remains a beautiful and effective weapon in the right hands.
