# The Forehand Volley

The forehand volley is the offensive weapon at net, typically used for put-aways and angle creation. It uses the continental grip for versatility between flat punches and slight underspin. The motion is a short, firm punch forward rather than a full swing, with the non-dominant hand often used to track the ball and maintain balance.
