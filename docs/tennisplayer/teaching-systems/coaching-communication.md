# Effective Coaching Communication

Effective coaches use concise, positive cues: 'toss high', 'low to high', 'split then push'. They focus on one adjustment at a time, prioritize feel over technical jargon, and ask the student to describe what they feel. The best coaches are 80% question-asker and 20% instructor.
