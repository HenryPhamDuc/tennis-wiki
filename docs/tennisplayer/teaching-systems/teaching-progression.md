# Tennis Teaching Progression

A structured progression moves students from beginner to advanced through carefully sequenced stages. The traditional progression: grips and stance, basic strokes from short court, rallying skills, point play, patterns, and finally tactical match play. Each stage builds on the previous and introduces new concepts at the right developmental moment.
