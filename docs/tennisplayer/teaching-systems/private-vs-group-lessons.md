# Private vs Group Lessons

Private lessons offer personalized attention and faster progress on specific issues, while group lessons provide more touches per hour, social pressure, and varied playing styles. The optimal mix depends on the student's level: beginners benefit from group foundations, intermediate players need private technical work, and advanced players need both tactical private sessions and competitive group play.
