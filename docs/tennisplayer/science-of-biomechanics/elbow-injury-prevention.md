# Elbow and Tennis Elbow Prevention

Tennis elbow (lateral epicondylitis) is common in tennis players. Causes: improper grip pressure, arm-dominant strokes, poor kinetic chain, and overuse. Prevention: develop kinetic chain usage, use appropriate grip pressure, strengthen forearm extensors, and use proper equipment (grip size, racquet weight).
