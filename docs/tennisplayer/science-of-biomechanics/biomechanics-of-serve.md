# Biomechanics of the Tennis Serve

The tennis serve is the most complex motion in sports biomechanics. It involves leg drive, hip rotation, shoulder internal rotation, elbow extension, wrist flexion, and pronation—all in less than half a second. The internal shoulder rotation alone can reach 7,000+ degrees per second, the fastest human motion recorded.
