# Shoulder Injury Prevention in Tennis

Tennis places enormous stress on the shoulder, particularly the rotator cuff. Prevention strategies: proper serving mechanics (avoid arm-only serving), strength training for the rotator cuff and scapular stabilizers, gradual volume increases, and listening to early warning signs. Shoulder injuries often start as fatigue or minor pain.
