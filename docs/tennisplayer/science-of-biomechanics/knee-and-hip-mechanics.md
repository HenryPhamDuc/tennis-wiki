# Knee and Hip Mechanics in Tennis

The hip is the powerhouse of tennis movement and stroke generation. Strong hip rotation and stability enable powerful groundstrokes, serves, and movement. The knees absorb landing forces and drive explosive direction changes. Hip and knee injuries are common in tennis, often from sudden direction changes or overuse.
