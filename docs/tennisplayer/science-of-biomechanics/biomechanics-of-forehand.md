# Biomechanics of the Forehand

Modern forehand biomechanics emphasize the kinetic chain and hip-shoulder separation. The pelvis rotates approximately 50-70 degrees, the shoulders 100+ degrees, and the arm whips through with racquet head speeds of 80+ mph at elite level. The pronation of the forearm at contact is critical for topspin generation.
