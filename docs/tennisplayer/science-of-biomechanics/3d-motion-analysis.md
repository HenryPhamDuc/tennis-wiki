# 3D Motion Analysis in Tennis

3D motion analysis uses high-speed cameras and reflective markers to capture the exact positions of body segments during tennis strokes. This research has revealed the precise biomechanics of elite serves, groundstrokes, and volleys, providing objective benchmarks for technique. The technology has democratized, with consumer-grade systems now available.
