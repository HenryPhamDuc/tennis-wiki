# Opponent Analysis and Scouting

Effective opponent analysis identifies weaknesses, patterns, and tendencies. Watch the warm-up: forehand grip, serve toss, movement patterns. Look for: weaker side (often backhand), preferred patterns, vulnerabilities under pressure, and physical limitations. Build your strategy around the opponent's weakness, not your strength.
