# Serve +1 Strategy

The serve +1 is the second shot after the serve, designed to put the opponent on the defensive. A wide slice serve pulls the opponent off court, opening the court for a forehand to the open space. A body serve jams the opponent, setting up a forehand to either side. The serve +1 wins more points than the serve itself.
