# Tactical Patterns in Tennis

Tactical patterns are pre-planned shot sequences that exploit court geometry, opponent weaknesses, and timing. Examples: serve-and-volley, serve-plus-one, baseline attrition, attack-the-backhand. The best players have 2-3 reliable patterns they can execute under pressure.
