# Match Statistics and Analytics

Modern tennis is increasingly data-driven. Key statistics include: first serve percentage, winners vs unforced errors, break point conversion, and shot placement heatmaps. Players can use post-match analytics to identify patterns and adjust strategy. Even amateur players benefit from tracking simple stats like first-serve percentage.
