# Focus and Concentration

Tennis focus oscillates between broad (whole court awareness) and narrow (specific point focus). The best players maintain 'soft focus': aware of the full court, opponent, and their own body, without fixating on any single element. Practice focusing on process (your own stroke) rather than outcome (winning the point).
