# Mental Toughness in Tennis

Mental toughness is the ability to maintain focus, confidence, and emotional control under pressure. It is not about being emotionless, but about channeling emotions productively. The components: self-belief, emotional regulation, focus control, and motivation. Mental toughness is trainable, not innate.
