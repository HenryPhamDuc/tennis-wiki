# Emotional Control Under Pressure

Emotional control is the ability to stay calm and clear-headed in high-pressure situations. Techniques: breath control (4-7-8 breathing), physical reset routines, and cognitive reframing ('this is a fun challenge' rather than 'I must win'). Accept that pressure is part of competition, then channel it.
