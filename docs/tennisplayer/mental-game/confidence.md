# Building Tennis Confidence

Confidence in tennis comes from preparation, not from positive self-talk alone. The formula: build a strong technical base, then accumulate evidence of success in practice. Confidence under pressure comes from having a reliable routine and trusting the trained stroke. False confidence collapses under stress; earned confidence holds.
