# Deliberate Practice

Deliberate practice is focused, structured practice with specific goals and immediate feedback, as opposed to casual repetition. The key elements: clear goals, full concentration, immediate correction, and progressive difficulty. Research shows that deliberate practice, more than innate talent, distinguishes experts from amateurs in any field.
