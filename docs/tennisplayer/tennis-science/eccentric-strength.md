# Eccentric Strength in Tennis

Eccentric strength (muscle lengthening under load) is critical for tennis: decelerating the racquet after contact, controlling landings from jumps, and stabilizing direction changes. Most tennis injuries occur during eccentric phases. Eccentric training (Nordic curls, slow eccentrics) builds resilience and reduces injury risk.
