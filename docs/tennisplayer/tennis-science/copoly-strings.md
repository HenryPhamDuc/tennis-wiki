# Copolyester Strings Explained

Copolyester (polyester) strings are the modern standard for advanced players. They offer excellent control, durability, and spin potential, but at the cost of power and comfort. Hybrid setups (poly in mains, natural gut or multifilament in crosses) blend poly control with gut feel. String tension affects playability significantly.
