# Pressure Training Games

Pressure training games create high-stakes scenarios in practice: must-win points, consequences for errors, audience involvement, or bet-style scoring. The goal is to normalize pressure so the player performs consistently in competition. Research shows players who train under simulated pressure make fewer errors in actual matches.
