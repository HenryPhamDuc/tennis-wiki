# Junior Tennis Games

Junior tennis games use age-appropriate themes, scoring, and equipment to engage young players. The best junior games disguise repetition as play: relay races with racquets, animal-themed movement drills, and character-based stroke stories. The goal is technical repetition without boring repetition.
