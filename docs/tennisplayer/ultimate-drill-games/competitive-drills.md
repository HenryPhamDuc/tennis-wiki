# Competitive Drill Games for Training

Drill games combine technical practice with competitive pressure, mimicking match conditions while reinforcing stroke patterns. The best drill games have clear scoring, point consequences, and progressive difficulty. Examples: 'King of the Court' rotations, target zones with bonus points, and timed challenges that simulate tiebreak scenarios.
