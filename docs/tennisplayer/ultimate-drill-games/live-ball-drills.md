# Live Ball Drills

Live ball drills use a coach or partner feeding balls in patterns that simulate match play while allowing repetition. Examples: alternating forehand/backhand patterns, deep-to-short progressions, and approach-shot + volley sequences. These drills bridge the gap between isolated stroke practice and full match play.
