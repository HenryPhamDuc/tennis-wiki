# Effective Warm-Up Routines

A proper warm-up progresses from general to specific: light cardio for blood flow, dynamic stretching for range of motion, footwork patterns for activation, mini-tennis for touch, baseline rallies for rhythm, and then specific stroke patterns. Skipping steps or jumping into full-intensity hitting is a common cause of early-match injuries.
