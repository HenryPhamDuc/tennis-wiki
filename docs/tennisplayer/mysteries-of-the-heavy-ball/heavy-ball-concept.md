# The Heavy Ball Concept

A 'heavy' ball is one that arrives at the opponent with significant pace AND topspin, making it difficult to handle. Heavy balls push opponents back, kick up high, and force defensive replies. Modern players generate heavy balls through proper kinetic chain sequencing, racquet head speed, and low-to-high swing paths.
