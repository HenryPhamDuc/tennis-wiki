# Spin Rates and Rotation Axes

Modern tennis features high spin rates: 2,500-3,500 rpm on top forehands, 4,000+ rpm on kick serves. The axis of rotation matters too: pure topspin spins vertically, slice spins horizontally. The combination of high pace and high spin creates the 'heavy' ball that dominates modern tennis.
