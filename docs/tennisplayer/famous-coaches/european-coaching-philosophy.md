# European Coaching Philosophy

European coaching traditions, particularly Spanish and French, emphasize technical precision, patience in building strokes, and tactical intelligence. The Spanish 'metodologia' builds players through repetitive structured drills before introducing match play. French tennis historically produced elegant tacticians like Noah and Santoro.
